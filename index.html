Context-sensitive advice

We don't rely on scripts or canned material. Our advice is strongly influenced by the context of the conversation. You also set the goal of the interaction. Whether it's gettin a 2nd date, coming up with a funny reply, or asking your crush out on a date, we'll help without judgement.

On-going support

We continue to provide support as your conversations progress.

Privacy

Any and all content you share on the app stays between you and your texting advisors. Hermes also has a built-in censorship tool to protect your and your conversational partner's identity.
